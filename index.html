What if my WhatsApp account gets banned?
Our compliance-first methodology is built specifically to prevent this. We warm up accounts gradually, monitor quality scores daily, and never blast unverified lists. In 2+ years of running campaigns, our clients have had zero permanent bans. If any issue arises, we resolve it at no extra cost.
💰
Isn't the ₹20,000–₹25,000 setup fee too high?
Compare it to one month of a cold calling team — that's ₹2,10,000+. The setup fee is a one-time cost that gets your entire WhatsApp infrastructure live. Most clients recover it within the first two weeks of their first campaign through lead quality alone.
🔒
How do I know my customer data is safe?
Your data never leaves your control. We operate through your own WhatsApp Business account — we don't store or share your contact lists. You own the account, the number, and the data. We are purely your operational partner.
📋
Is WhatsApp marketing legal for financial products in India?
Yes, with proper opt-in and template compliance. We help you build opt-in flows, get templates pre-approved by Meta, and follow all TRAI and Meta guidelines for financial services messaging. We've run compliant campaigns for credit cards, loans, mutual funds, and insurance DSAs.
